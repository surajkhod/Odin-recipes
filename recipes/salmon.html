Super simple Salmon

Description

This easy salmon recipe is a delicious way to prepare fresh fillets using just a few ingredients from your pantry.

Ingredients :

  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps :

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl.

  3. Step 3

    4. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.

  4. Step 4

    5. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.

  5. Step 5

    6. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
