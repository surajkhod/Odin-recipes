Pan-Fried Pork Chops with Sweet BBQ Sauce

Description

Pork chops are cool again and this pan-fried version with barbecue sauce is addictive and ready in 15 minutes.

Ingredients :

  • 1 pound (1/2-inch thick) pork chops
  • kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • Â½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, or to taste

Steps :

  1. Step 1

    2. Season pork chops on both sides with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add pork chops in a single layer; cook until golden, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

  2. Step 2

    3. Meanwhile, whisk barbecue sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, and garlic powder together in a bowl; pour over seared pork chops and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium, cover, and simmer until pork chops cooked through, 4 to 5 minutes. Garnish with parsley.
